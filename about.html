About Us

Well sueeeeeey!--welcome to our little hole in the web, website.

The Millard Barbeque Company is a family-owned business owned by Sue and Doug Pfefferwallas of Plattsmouth, Nebraska.

Why Barbeque? Well, why not? Look, you can't deny the allure of slow smoked pork, drenched in Barbeque sauce--well, at least we can't. As big fans of Kansas City Joe's, we wanted to bring some smoky magic to Husker country. In the Summer of 2010, we purchased a smoker and a food truck and history was made. Please stop on in for some Barbeque that will make you shake in your boots.
